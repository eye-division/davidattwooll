David Attwooll

David Attwooll’s first full collection, The Sound Ladder, was published in April 2015 by Two Rivers Press. His work has appeared in several magazines and anthologies, and he was one of the winners of the 2013 Poetry Business pamphlet prize with Surfacing (smith/doorstop). Ground Work (Black Poplar, 2014) followed, a pamphlet illustrated by Andrew Walton.

David lives in Oxford where he works in publishing and drums in a street band.

Read a selection of his poetry online, reviews and critiques, find out about forthcoming and past readings, get in touch, and purchase his book or pamphlets.
